About

Hi I’m Jane, I am a graduate from Parsons School of Design with an MFA in Design and Technology. Currently I work as a User Experience designer for GoHealth.

My interests are mission driven and center around expanding health access and education, developing inclusive designs, and exploring the intersection of science, design, and technology.

I am a part-time traveler, a full-time eater, a one-time pro soccer player, a big-time nature enthusiast, a budding gardener and an amateur mycologist.
